Whether you're an old player, a new player, or a little bit of both, we welcome all active players who want to join a community that play Eve like the game it is. Pet Traders Goals

We strive to build a strong community based on mutual support and cooperation. We organize joint operations, help new players develop, and together explore the possibilities that Eve Online offers us.

What even is a Pet Trader?

We love our PvP players! Most of us are industrialists, too! We have an entire region of sovereign nullsec to call our home, not far from Jita, and with everything you need. Our alliance has R64 moon pops open to everyone, a gamut of ore anomalies, ice, you name it! And a full industrial park, for all that entails.

GPT is primarily an EUTZ-based corp, but our USTZ contingent isn't bad, either. Our alliance is primarily USTZ, and we have a tight-knit, active relationship with SLOW, as well.

  • We have a 90% general buyback
  • We do organized fleets frequently, for mining with boosts, and for PvP, both defensive and offensive, and for fun.
  • We have weekly Sunday School lessons for all the new guys (and even the old guy writing this post needs to attend more of those).
